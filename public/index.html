About me

I'm a software engineer with 2 1/2 years experience at Common Knowledge - a co-operative that uses technology to help social movements build power

I graduated from the full stack web development bootcamp Founders and Coders in 2021. I completed a Software developer (level 4) apprenticeship with a merit in 2023

Prior to that I worked in book publishing where I won a London Book Fair Trailblazer award.
