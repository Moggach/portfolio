About me

I'm a junior software engineer with two years experience at Common Knowledge - a co-operative that uses technology to help social movements build power

I graduated from the full stack web development bootcamp Founders and Coders in 2021.

Prior to that I worked in book publishing where I won a London Book Fair Trailblazer award.

I also work for the National Domestic Abuse Helpline providing support and information to survivors of domestic abuse and other forms of violence against women and girls.
