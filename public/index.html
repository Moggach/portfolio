About me

I'm a software engineer at Policy in Practice working on their award winning Better Off benefits calculator

Previously I worked at Common Knowledge - a co-operative that uses technology to help social movements build power

I graduated from the full stack web development bootcamp Founders and Coders in 2021. I completed a Software developer (level 4) apprenticeship with a merit in 2023

I also work for the National Domestic Abuse Helpline providing support and information to survivors of domestic abuse and other forms of violence against women and girls.

I built and maintain a website to help professionals and survivors find their local domestic abuse support service. The code is open source and PRs are welcome!

Have a question or want to work together? Get in touch at anna_cunnane@proton.me
